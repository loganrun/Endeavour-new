GAMER'S CAMP

Create Your Own Video Games!
July 2015

Anyone can learn to code. In a few hours your child will pick up the basic skills and in a few weeks they will be able to build websites.

Your child will learn the basics for using Adobe's powerful graphics suite to create their favorite characters. They will then learn Construct2 to make those characters come to life. Your future computer engineers will also learn how to write a story and animate it. Finally, they will build a website to share their creations with the world. They will never forget this summer camp!

More details will be available shortly. Please add your contact information to our list and we will send you additional information as it becomes available. Thank you and see you this summer!
